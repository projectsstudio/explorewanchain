What is WAN?

WAN or Wancoin is the native currancy of Wanchain. All Wanchain transactions, cross chain transactions, and smart contracts are powered by WAN. WAN will also used be used as stake in Wanchain's upcoming Proof of Stake system, and also serves as bond for Wanchain's cross chain Storeman nodes. WAN and Wanchain based tokens are NOT ERC-20 tokens. Do not send any WAN or WRC-20 tokens to an Ethereum address or they will be lost forever.
